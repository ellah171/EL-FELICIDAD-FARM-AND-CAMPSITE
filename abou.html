Welcome to El Felicidad

El Felicidad is a special campsite where you can enjoy nature, meet friendly animals, and explore a farm full of fruits and vegetables. We offer tent accommodations and a cozy treehouse with beautiful views of a rice field. Learn more about the fun activities and relaxing stays we offer.

Our Goal

We aim to give our guests a happy and peaceful experience. This website shares everything you need to know about our farm, animals, and accommodations to help you plan your visit.

Seasonal Changes

Some activities and accommodations may change depending on the season, but there's always something wonderful to enjoy at El Felicidad.

What You'll Find Here

  • Exciting activities like animal interactions and picnics.
  • Information about tent stays and treehouse views.
  • Images of our site and practical details like pricing and location.
